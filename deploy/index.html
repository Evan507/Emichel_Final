Autumn Happenings

It is autumn in the marsh, and The Wetlands Institute is open Saturdays and Sundays from 9:30-4:30, as conditions allow. Unless otherwise stated, all activities are free with admission and occur onsite at The Wetlands Institute.

Winter Happenings

Even though it might be cold outside, it is always warm inside at The Wetlands Institute! Please join us for activities on the marsh every Saturday and Sunday from September 23, 2019 until May 18, 2020. Unless otherwise stated, all activities are included in the price of admission and occur onsite at The Wetlands Institute.

Spring Happenings

The Wetlands Institute is currently closed. Questions? Please call The Wetlands Institute at 609-368-1211. Please note: Summer hours and activities start June 22, 2020. As permissible by executive order, The Wetlands Institute will be open daily from 9:30am to 4:30pm with special evening hours until 7pm Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday.

Summer Happenings

We can’t wait to see you again! After three long months, The Wetlands Institute re-opened on Wednesday, July 1st, and will be open daily from 9:30-4:30. As you prepare for your visit, just a few quick rules and procedures to ensure your visit is safe for your family and ours.
